## Mainnets and Testnets
Mainnets are where bitcoin, ethereum, etc. and all other real crypto live. Testnets is the place where developers test out smart contracts

## Oracles
Any service or device that delivers data to blockchains. DON- Decentralised Oracle Network. Examples of DON include ChainLink

## Ethereum Virtual Machine
It is a standard that defines how smart contracts should execute. It is the stuff that runs smart contracts on the Ethereum block chain and its L2s
