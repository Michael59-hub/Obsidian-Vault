## Pharmacokinetics
- well absorbed

- Long half-lives

- Once daily at night because of their sedating effects.

- Extensive metabolism via demethylation, aromatic hydroxylation, and glucuronide conjugation.

- Only about 5% of TCAs are excreted unchanged in the urine


## MOA
They inhibit [[SERT (Serotonin Transporter)]], [[NET (Norepinephrine Transporter)]] and also have effects on cholinergic, histamine and other receptors.
