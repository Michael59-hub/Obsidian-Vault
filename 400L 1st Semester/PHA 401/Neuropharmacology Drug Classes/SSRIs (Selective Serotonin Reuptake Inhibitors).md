## Pharmacokinetics
Highly lipophilic as with all antidepressants(they have to pass the BBB).

## Pharmacodynamics
At therapeutic doses, about 80% of the activity of the transporter is inhibited.

## MOA
They bind allosterically to [[SERT (Serotonin Transporter)]] at the presynaptic neuron and inhibit the reuptake of serotonin

## Notes
- High affinity for monoamine receptors
- They seem to inhibit the dopaminergic system though the is high variability with this feature.

1. [[Fluoxetine]]
2. [[Escitalopram]]
