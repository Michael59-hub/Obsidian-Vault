## Pharmacokinetics
- All the venlafaxine stuff
- Unlike most antidepressants, desvenlafaxine is conjugated and does not undergo extensive oxidative metabolism.
- At least 45% of desvenlafaxine is excreted unchanged in the urine compared with 4–8% of venlafaxine.
