## Pharmacokinetics
- Unicyclic aminoketone structure.

- Its unique structure results in a different side-effect profile than most antidepressants

- Resembles amphetamine in chemical structure and, like the stimulant.

- Rapidly absorbed and has a mean protein binding of 85%.

- It undergoes extensive hepatic metabolism and has a substantial first-pass effect.

- It has three active metabolites including hydroxybupropion; the latter is being developed as an antidepressant.

- Biphasic elimination with the first phase lasting about 1 hour and the second phase lasting 14 hours.
