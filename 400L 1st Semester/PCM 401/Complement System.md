This is a group of 20 proteins that act in concert to enhance the actions of other defence mechanisms.
They are produced by liver cells and macrophages
## Complement Activation Pathways
### 1. Classical Pathway
- Initiated by antigen-antibody complexes
- Activated most effectively by IgG and IgM
- C1 interacts with the FC portion of a bound antibody which would lead to the cleaving of C1
- Cleaving of C1 produces a cascade of cleaving in the proteins till it reaches C3(the most abundant/important)
- C3 is where classical and alternative pathways converge
- C3 is the cleaved into C3a (large active form) and C3b (fragment)

### 2. Alternative Pathway
- Initiated by pathogens and tumor cells


## Functions of Complement System
1. Cell lysis. They pierce the outer membrane of pathogens
2. Opsonisation. They make pathogens more attractive to phagocytic cells such as macrophages.
3. Inflammation. They promote the release of histamine.
