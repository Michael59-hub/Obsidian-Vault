## MOA
They cause excess release of dopamine and noradrenaline by three main actions:
### 1. Enter the presynaptic neuron

- Taken up via **DAT and NET** (dopamine & norepinephrine transporters)
    

---

### 2. Displace monoamines from vesicles

- Enter synaptic vesicles via **VMAT2**
    
- **Displace DA/NE from storage vesicles** into the cytoplasm
    

---

### 3. Reverse monoamine transporters

- High cytoplasmic DA/NE causes **DAT and NET to run in reverse**
    
- Result → **non-exocytotic release** of DA/NE into the synaptic cleft
