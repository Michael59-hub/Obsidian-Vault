## Pharmacokinetics 
- Its primary metabolites, hydroxynefazodone and m-cpp are both inhibitors of the 5-HT 2 receptor.

- Hepatotoxic

- Extensive metabolism

- Extensively bound to protein and have limited bioavailability because of extensive metabolism.

- Short half-lives generally require split dosing

- Potent inhibitor of [[CYP3A4]]
