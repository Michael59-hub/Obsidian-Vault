## Pharmacokinetics
-  Rapidly absorbed with protein binding of about 85%.

- The half-life is variable, and the drug is often given in divided doses.

- Extensive hepatic metabolism.

- One of the active metabolites, 7-hydroxyamoxapine, is a potent D 2 blocker and is associated with antipsychotic effects.
