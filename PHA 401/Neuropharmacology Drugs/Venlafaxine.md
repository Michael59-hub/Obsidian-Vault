## Pharmacokinetics
- Venlafaxine is extensively metabolized in the liver via the CYP2D6 isoenzyme to O -desmethylvenlafaxine ([[Desvenlafaxine]]).

- Both have similar half-lives of about 11 hours.

- Despite the relatively short half-lives, both drugs are available in formulations that allow once-daily dosing. ==Venlafaxine and desvenlafaxine have the lowest protein binding of all antidepressants (27–30%)==.
