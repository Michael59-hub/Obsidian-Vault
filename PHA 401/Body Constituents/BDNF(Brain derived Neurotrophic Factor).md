**Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor (BDNF)** is a vital protein that acts like "Miracle-Gro" for your brain. It belongs to a family of growth factors called neurotrophins that help regulate the survival, development, and function of neurons.

While most of the brain is formed before birth, BDNF is one of the primary reasons the adult brain remains **plastic**—meaning it can change, learn, and repair itself throughout your life.

BDNF is thought to exert its influence on neuronal survival and growth effects by activating the ==**tyrosine kinase receptor B**== in both neurons and glia.
